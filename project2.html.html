Tank bund


The tank bund is dotted with elegant statues of eminent historic personalities such as Nannayya, Tikkana, Molla, Sri Sri, Jashua, Annamayya, Tyagayya, Vemana Errana, Rudramma, and Pingali Venkaiah greeting you warmly. The Tank Bund, which is the dam/embankment to the Hussain Sagar, serves as the link between the twin cities of Hyderabad and Secunderabad. It was built by Hazrat Hussain Shah, and today it is a major tourist attraction. A monolithic statue of the Buddha installed with pain staking efforts in the heart of this lake is an added charm. The lake was excavated in the year1562 A.D. by Hussain Shah Wali during the reign of Ibrahim Quli Qutb Shah, the promenade of which is now a busy thoroughfare. Boating and water sports are a regular feature in the Hussain Sagar Lake. It is a large manmade lake of Hyderabad and was constructed on a small tributary of the Musi River. It is a sprawling lake that holds water in all seasons. The Department of culture organizes cultural programmes here regularly.A Historical Marvel Tank Bund has a rich history that dates back to the reign of the Qutb Shahi dynasty in the 16th century. The bund (embankment) was built to prevent the flooding of the city and provide a source of water for irrigation. It was during the rule of Ibrahim Quli Qutb Shah, the fourth ruler of the Qutb Shahi dynasty, that the construction of the bund commenced. Over the years, various renovations and enhancements transformed it into the captivating attraction it is today.

Architectural Marvel and Construction Details

Tank Bund, the architectural marvel in Hyderabad, was designed and constructed under the supervision of Sir Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya, a renowned engineer and statesman of India. The construction of Tank Bund began in 1923 and was completed in 1930, making it an impressive engineering feat of its time. The project aimed to create an embankment that would regulate the water flow and prevent flooding in the city. It involved extensive planning, engineering expertise, and collaboration with skilled laborers. The total cost of the construction was around 2.5 million Indian rupees, a significant investment for that era. The architectural brilliance of Tank Bund showcases the ingenuity and vision of its creators, leaving a lasting impression on both locals and travelers alike.

Aesthetic Charm and Iconic Statues

One of the standout features of Tank Bund is its aesthetic charm. The bund stretches across Hussain Sagar Lake and offers breathtaking views of the water body and its surroundings. The mesmerizing sunset view from Tank Bund is a sight to behold, as the sun's golden hues reflect off the tranquil waters, creating a picturesque ambiance. Another notable attraction is the installation of iconic statues of renowned personalities. The most prominent among them is the monolithic statue of Gautam Buddha, which stands tall on a small island in the middle of the lake. This magnificent statue adds a spiritual touch to the atmosphere and is a symbol of peace and tranquility.

Recreational Activities and Entertainment

Tank Bund offers a range of recreational activities and entertainment options for visitors. The picturesque surroundings provide an ideal setting for a leisurely stroll or a jog along the bund. The well-maintained pathways and lush green gardens on either side of the bund enhance the overall experience. Boating is another popular activity on Hussain Sagar Lake. Visitors can rent paddleboats, motorboats, or even larger vessels to explore the lake and enjoy the serene environment. The boat rides offer a unique perspective of the surrounding attractions, including Tank Bund and its statues. For those interested in cultural experiences, Tank Bund often hosts various events and exhibitions that showcase the local art and craftsmanship of Hyderabad. These events provide a platform for local artisans to display their talent and offer visitors a chance to immerse themselves in the vibrant culture of the city.

The Necklace Road Connection

Tank Bund is seamlessly connected to Necklace Road, a vibrant boulevard that extends from the bund to the neighboring areas. Necklace Road is lined with recreational spots, including parks, restaurants, cafes, and shopping centers. Visitors can take a leisurely walk along the road, enjoy the cool breeze, and savor delicious local cuisine at the numerous food stalls and restaurants. The connectivity between Tank Bund and Necklace Road makes it convenient for travelers to explore both attractions without any hassle. Whether it's a peaceful evening walk or a delightful culinary journey, the combination of Tank Bund and Necklace Road offers a complete experience for travelers in Hyderabad.
